About Sabby

Located in what used to be a bánh mì place on Victoria Parade in Collingwood, Sabby is a gathering place by and for artists, game makers, crafters and zinesters of Narrm (Melbourne).

Sabby is open on Mondays and Tuesdays from 11 a.m. to 6 p.m., as well as during any public event.

What Sabby does

Sabby is a co-working space, a gallery space, an events and functions space, a casual hang out space.

Sabby strives to be a safe and inclusive space that can be enjoyed by everyone.

Where is Sabby?

Sabby is located at:
141 Victoria Parade, Collingwood.

You can get to Sabby via:

  • Tram: 12 and 109, Stop 16 - Wellington St (2 mins walk)
  • Train: Hurstbridge and Mernda lines, North Richmond Station (7 min walk)
